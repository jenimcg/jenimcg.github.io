About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Jeni McGuigan - an email geek. I have 10+ years of design experience, including over 8 years specific to email design, templating and development. I've managed the email template library at AWeber, refreshing the entire library as well as adding creative templates to the Landing Page builder.

In addition, I created countless email designs and templates for users on AWeber, and provided feedback on coding best practices for custom email designs along with campaign feedback and best practices to better reach their audiences.

Most importantly I'm a mother to 2 amazing little girls and wife to a phenomenal husband. We love taking part in our local community events and supporting small businesses. We also have two crazy dogs; a shiba inu and rescue mutt.

Ouside of email, I enjoy taking walks and hikes at local parks, especially those with water and waterfalls for the girls and pups to play in.
